Automated combat audio for Foundry VTT.

Resonance listens to chat messages, attack rolls, damage applications, and combat workflows, then triggers the appropriate sound effect automatically. Works with local sound files (WAV/MP3) out of the box. Optionally connects to Syrinscape for cloud-hosted audio.

 

✨ New in v2.2.0

  • 389 SFX Pack sounds included — local combat, weapon, monster, spell, and stinger audio. Select "Ionrift SFX Pack" preset to activate. No accounts required.
  • Configurable Sound Timing — tune the delay between impact and pain vocals, AoE stagger, crit decorations, and more via the Orchestrator UI.
  • Polished configuration UIs — clearer labels, humanized picker titles, conditional Voice Tone display, and improved sound picker with distinct browse vs. playlist sections.

 

How it works

  • Sound resolution — follows a priority waterfall: item/actor overrides → monster families → weapon categories → core fallbacks. First match wins.
  • Weapon and spell detection — resolves sounds by weapon name (Sword, Dagger, Bow), damage type (Slashing, Bludgeoning, Piercing), and spell school (Evocation, Necromancy, etc.).
  • Monster classification — identifies creature families (Dragon, Undead, Beast) from actor names and assigns sounds per family.
  • Per-actor and per-item overrides — every actor and item sheet has a 🎵 Sounds button. Custom bindings always take priority over system defaults.
  • Critical hits, pain, death — distinct stingers for crits, pain vocals on damage (masculine/feminine), and death sounds when HP reaches zero.
  • Orchestration — budget windows prevent sound spam. Configurable timing offsets control the stagger between layered sounds.

 

Supported systems

  • Daggerheart (native) — Hope/Fear mechanics, Duality Dice rolls, Fear Tracker thresholds, and resource changes all have sound hooks. No additional modules needed.
  • DnD5e (via Midi-QOL) — full attack/damage/healing workflow support. Two-beat sequences: weapon swing, then hit or miss result.

 

Resonance Calibration

Configuration UI for mapping game events to sound bindings. Three tiers: Core events → Weapon categories → Monster families. Per-actor and per-item overrides take priority over all tiers.

Requirements

  • Ionrift Library — required dependency.
  • Midi-QOL — required for DnD5e automation. Not needed for Daggerheart.
